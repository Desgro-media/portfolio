The Journey

HOW IT
BEGAN

2023 — FRIBOURG, SWITZERLAND

THE SEED

Two founders completed their Masters in Marketing at a quiet Swiss university town. Late nights, big ideas, and a shared obsession with brand storytelling planted the seed for what would become DesGro.

2023 — DUBAI, UAE

THE CATALYST

Mentored by Mahdi Shafiei of Trifid Media — the biggest marketing agency in the GCC — the founders gained frontline insight into elite-level brand strategy and what it takes to build agencies that last.

2024 — CALICUT, INDIA

THE LAUNCH

Armed with a world-class education and Dubai-forged instincts, DesGro was born at Hilite Business Park, Calicut — starting with a founding team of just 3.

2025–2026 — SCALING FAST

THE MOVEMENT

What started with 3 is now a powerhouse of 40+ creators, strategists and marketers across 7+ departments — 150+ creators built, 100M+ views generated, and still accelerating.
